Bloodline: Fairy

SP:Lightning Strike,SP:Dragon's Breath,

S200,S500,S1450,S1500,

C105,C250,C400,C1300,

D200,D290,D330,D1275,

E135,E320,E590,E1225,

A120,A270,A305,A1500,

W275,W400,W1275,W1375

Note: Buff W275 before running this build.

Note: Estimated 10 minutes for Secrets of the Warrior to unlock. Use Research template to get Fairy Lineage 25 afterwards.

Build has 3 stages:

1: Gather faction coins for 400 Fairy Exchanges and buy Fairy Lineage (Cast Lightning Strike and Dragon's Breath on bronze, Tax Collection on gold).

2: Buy Freemason's Hall (1e125 / 100 Qag), Appraisal Vantage (1e135 / 1 Qaqag) and cast all spells on bronze at the highest tier, Tax Collection/ Appraisal Vantage on gold.

3: Buy Secrets of the Warrior, load the research build and get Fairy Lineage level 25.

Note: Know Your Enemy, Part II Lore Artifact (R75)

After the Mercenary Encampment Quest (Merc Unique Building unlock) is bought, Know Your Enemy, Part II Artifact will become available to be excavated.

Requirement: R75+, Merc Unique Building, play as mercenary and have upgrades from all 12 other factions.

Chance: 5%

Effect: Awards an upgrade of the same name.

Tip: Dragon Bloodline helps to afford a Dragon upgrade easier.
