In-game description

The wise druids of old were a group of people from all races who strove to maintain world balance with mystical discipline and powerful magic.

Affiliating yourself with the Druids faction will boost your magic capabilities and reward balancing of all things.

Alignment: Neutral

The Druids feature a powerful Heritage that increases maximum mana, and their final Challenge reward extends their spell to affect more buildings.

Unlock Requirements:

Fossilized Piece of Bark 1

Description: We discovered a fossilized piece of bark with the image of a Faction Coin carved into it.

Type: Quest Artifact

Requirement: Have at least 1 B (1e9) gems in the abdication treasury, 10 Excavations

Description: 1st clue required to unlock the Druid Alliance.


Fossilized Piece of Bark 2

Description: We discovered a fossilized piece of bark with the symbol of One Million.

Type: Quest Artifact

Requirement: Have at least 1 B (1e9) gems in the abdication treasury, 25 Excavations

Description: 2nd clue required to unlock the Druid Alliance.


Druidic Runestone

Type: Upgrade

Requirement: 2 Fossilized Pieces of Bark, 1 M (1e6) Faction Coins (This Game)

Cost: 1 No (1e30), 25,000 Elf Coins, 25,000 Demon Coins


Faction spell

Grand Balance

Cost: 1,000 Mana - Duration: 20 seconds

Effect: Increase the production of your least productive building based on the amount of the three most built ones.

Formula: (25 * (x * y * z / (A + 3) ^ 6) ^ 0.9)%, where x, y and z are the number of your three most-built buildings and A is how many times you have ascended.


Spell Trophy & Upgrade

Halls of Balance

Requirement: Have Grand Balance affect Hall of Legends.

Cost: 10 Dd (1e40), A1+ Free

Effect: Increases Grand Balance duration to 24 seconds and lower its cost by 200 mana.

Proof Of Neutrality

Type: Alignment Upgrade

Requirement: Collect the 2 quest artifacts of any Neutral Faction.

Cost: 10Qa (1e16)

Note: To get 1 M (1e6) Faction Coins use the Elf faction with the Elven Luck upgrade (up to 100% chance of faction coins), Goblin with the Goblin Greed upgrade, or Titan for the Lightning Strike spell.

In order to affiliate with a Neutral Faction, like for Vanilla Factions, you first need to choose the alignment. The alignment upgrade for Neutral Factions is called the Proof of Neutrality.

To get Proof of Neutrality you have to unlock at least 1 of the neutral factions.

Tier 1 Upgrades

Druids Trade Treaty

Cost: 250 Elven Coins + 250 Demon Coins

Effect: Unlocks Tier 1 Druid upgrades.


Druidic Vocabulary

Cost: 500 Qi (5e20)

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on their tier, giving the best bonus to the lowest.

Formula: (4000 * (12 - T))%, where T is building tier.


Animal Companions

Cost: 5 Sx (5e21)

Effect: You instantly attract 3 Animal assistants and more will come based on the time spent in this game.

Formula: +(3 + 1.75 * x ^ 0.5), where x is time spent in this game.


Natural Recycling

Cost: 50 Sx (5e22)

Effect: Increase Faction Coin find chance additively and multiplicatively based on the amount of the total buildings you own.

Formula: (0.4 x ^ 0.4)%, where x is total buildings built. (additively and multiplicatively)

Tier 2 Upgrades

Druids Friendship Pact

Cost: 2,500 Elven Coins + 2,500 Demon Coins

Effect: Unlocks Tier 2 Druid Upgrades.


Earthly Bond

Cost: 5 Sp (5e24)

Effect: Increase maximum Mana additively based on the highest amount of Stone Pillars built in this Reincarnation.

Formula: +(2 * x), where x is the highest amount of Stone Pillars built in this Reincarnation.


Bardic Knowledge

Cost: 50 Sp (5e25)

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on your Maximum Mana.

Formula: (16 * ln(1 + x) ^ 2)%, where x is your Maximum Mana.


Shapeshifting

Cost: 500 Sp (5e26)

Effect: Increase Mana Regeneration additively based on the amount of assistants you own.

Formula: +(1.3 * x ^ 0.7), where x is the amount of assistants you own.

Tier 3 Upgrades

Druids Alliance

Cost: 10,000 Elven Coins + 10,000 Demon Coins

Effect: Unlocks Tier 3 Druid upgrades.


Mabinogion

Cost: 50 Oc (5e28)

Effect: Increase the production of Non-Unique buildings based on their tier, giving the best bonus to the lowest.

Formula: (12 * 1.8 ^ (12 - T))%, where T is building tier.


Earthly Soul

Cost: 500 Oc (5e29)

Effect: Increase Mana Regeneration additively based on the highest amount of Stone Pillars you built in this Reincarnation.

Formula: +(x ^ 0.25), where x is the highest amount of Stone Pillars you built in this Reincarnation.


Building Jungle

Cost: 5 No (5e30)

Effect: Increase the production of each building based on the amount of buildings of the same type.

Formula: (1.5 * x ^ 0.9)%, where x is the amount of buildings of the same tier.

Druid Heritage

Requirement: Druid Champion Trophy

Cost: 25,000 Elven Coins, 25,000 Demon Coins

Effect: Increase maximum Mana by 1 every 15 buildings you own.

Druid Research Requirement

Stonehenge Circles Quest

Description: Hello, friend. The balance of nature requires more magic to sustain it. Build enough Stone Pillars, we'll gather our elders to improve them.

Requirement: R22, 2,000 Stone Pillars

Cost: 10 Dtg (1e100)


Stonehenge Circles

Upgrade Stone Pillars to Stonehenge Circles, boosting their production based on Maximum Mana and unlocking more unique perks for the building.

Formula: (30 * x ^ 0.6)%, where x is your Maximum Mana.

Cost: 10 Ttg (1e103)

Effect: Also allows access to the Research Facilities(after completing Secrets of the Ancients).


Key to the Lost City

Description: Despite being thousands of years old, it's still shiny.

Requirement: Found on the 1,500th excavation, R23+

Effect: Unlocks the Secrets of the Ancients.


Secrets of the Ancients

Requirement: Key To The Lost City artifact, Any Neutral Unique Building.

Cost: 10 Noqag (1e151) Coins, 1 M (1e6) Fairy, Elven, Angel, Goblin, Undead, Demon coins

Effect: Unlocks research for Titan, Druids and Faceless and increases production by 100,000%, plus an additional bonus based on Economics research points.

Note: Required once throughout the entire game play and does not count towards the bought upgrade stat.


Ancient Device

Description: This strange device seems to react to the Ancient Races magical capabilities... We may channel its power to increase their research potential!

Type: Quest Artifact and Upgrade

Requirement: Play any Neutral Faction, have their Unique Building and over 2,000 excavations.

Cost: 100 QiSxg (1e200)

Effect: Adds 2 extra slots to the Neutral faction you are playing.

Note: Druid research is related to Economics and Alchemy

Chance: 0.2% per excavation


Unique Building

Ascension 2 - R100+

Additional Alignment: Balance


Ziggurat Quest

Description: Hello, friend. We are close to restore the world balance... build us more Stonehenges, and we'll upgrade other buildings to be great places of power.

Requirement: 10,000 Stonehenges

Cost: 1 Ud (1e36) Emerald Coins

Effect: Grants access to Unique Building.


Unique Building Upgrade

Requirement: Ziggurat Quest

Cost: 1 Td (1e42) Emerald Coins

Effect: Gives Ziggurat Unique Building.


Unique Building

Upgrade Ancient Pyramids to Ziggurats, boosting their production based on lineage levels and unlocking more unique perks for the building.

Effect: Increase production based on total Lineage levels.

Formula: floor(x ^ 1.1)%, where x is total level of Lineages.

Effect: Also grants access to Faction Union.

Tier 4 Upgrades

Druids Union

Requirement: Ziggurat Unique Building

Cost: 1 Qi (1e18) Elven and Demon Coins

Effect: Grants access to Union Upgrades.

Effect (R111+): Increase the production of Non-Unique buildings based on your total Lineage level and the maximum amount of Grand Balance targets.

Formula: (2.4 * x ^ 1.025 * y ^ 1.2)%, where x is total Lineage levels and y is amount of buildings targeted by Grand Balance (maximum is 11).


Building Vines

Requirement: Druids Union

Cost: 1 Nod (1e60) Emerald Coins

Effect: Increase spell duration based on the highest amount of Ziggurats you built in this Reincarnation.

Formula: (0.5 * x ^ 0.5)%, where x is the highest amount of Ziggurats built in this Reincarnation.


Lunar Cycle

Requirement: Druids Union

Cost: 100 Qivg (1e80) Emerald Coins

Effect: Increase Maximum Mana additively based on time spent online in this game.

Formula: +(50 * x ^ 0.85), where x is time spent online in this game.

Effect: Increase Maximum Mana multiplicatively based on time spent offline in this game.

Formula: (0.5 * x ^ 0.5)%, where x is time spent offline in this game.


Grove Farming

Requirement: Druids Union

Cost: 100 Tg (1e95) Emerald Coins

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on their tier. Middle tiers get better bonuses.

Formula: (0.8 * ((6 - abs(6 - T)) ^ 4))%, where T is building tier.

Advanced Druid Heritage

Requirement: Druid Master trophy and 6 hours activity time (This Game) of Grand Balance.

Cost: 1 Sp (1e24) Elven and Demon Coins

Effect: Multiplicatively increase Faction Coin find chance based on the sum of all your spells durations.

Formula: (9 * x ^ 0.3)%, where x is the sum of all your spell durations.

Note: When using their lineage, Advanced Heritage powers are multiplied by the level of the lineage.

Formula: *(1 + 0.3 * level)
