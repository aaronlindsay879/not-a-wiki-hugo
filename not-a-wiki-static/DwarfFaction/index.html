In-game description

The Dwarves make the best masons, artisans and blacksmiths in all the realms. Their creations will outlast anything made by other races... except for beer, that is.

Alignment: Good

The Dwarven Faction appears in addition to a good faction and enables the player to use all the 2 faction spells and 18 upgrades from both the Good and Dwarven factions.

The Dwarven faction cannot be combined with Evil or Neutral Factions, nor is there a Research Tree for Dwarven.

Unlock Requirements:

These Requirements can be done in separate game plays.

Passage to the Underworld

Requirements: 200 Excavations

Cost: 50 Qid (5e49)

Effect: Unlocks Prestige Quests

Note: Only needs to be bought once and do not count towards the 'Bought Upgrades' stats.


Sturdy Bearded Man's Quest

Requirements: Play a Good Alignment

Requirements: Build 800 Inns, 7,000 Buildings Owned (This Game), 10,000 clicks (This Reincarnation)

Cost: 10 Qad (1e46), 10,000 Fairy Coins, 10,000 Elven Coins, 10,000 Angel Coins

Effect: Unlocks Underworld Diplomacy

Note: Only needs to be done once and do not count towards the 'Bought Upgrades' stats.


Underworld Diplomacy

Requirements: Sturdy Bearded Man's Quest

Cost: 1Qad (1e45)

Effect: Unlocks Prestige Faction Coins, Trade Treaty, Royal Exchanges and Heritage, Required each run until R3


Faction spell

Diamond Pickaxe

Effect: Increase clicking reward by 25% per Excavation and "Faction Coin Find Chance" by +200% for 12 seconds.


Spell Trophy & Upgrade

Minedwarf

Requirement: Find 30,000 Faction Coins using Diamond Pickaxe in a single game.

Effect: Increases Diamond Pickaxe bonus to "Faction Coin Find Chance" to +300%.

Cost: 100 Sxd (1e53), A1+ Free

Tier 1 Upgrades

Dwarven Trade Treaty

Cost: 3,000 Dwarven Coins

Effect: Unlocks Tier 1 Upgrades


Dwarven Ale

Cost: 5 Qad (5e45)

Effect:Increase Faction Coin find chance based on the amount of Blacksmiths you own (Additive).

Formula: +(2 * x ^ 0.5)%, where x is the amount of Blacksmiths you own.


Expert Masonry

Cost: 50 Qad (5e46)

Effect: Reduce all building cost multipliers.

Effect: Reduces cost multiplier by 0.01; with no other reductions applying, the multiplier will be 1.14 instead of 1.15.


Mining Prodigies

Cost: 500 Qad (5e47)

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on the amount of Excavations you made, including resets, in this Reincarnation.

Formula: (40 * (x * (1 + 2 * y)) ^ 0.4)%, where x is the number of excavations made this Reincarnation, y is the number of excavation resets in this Reincarnation.

Tier 2 Upgrades

Dwarven Friendship Pact

Cost: 12,000 Dwarven Coins

Effect: Unlocks more Dwarven Upgrades.


Underground Citadels

Cost: 50 Qid (5e49)

Effect: Multiplicatively increase Mana Regeneration based on the amount of Gems you own.

Formula: (1.5 * log10(1 + x) ^ 1.5)%, where x is the amount of Gems you own.


Indestructible Treasure

Cost: 500 Qid (5e50)

Effect: Increase clicking reward based on the amount of buildings you own.

Formula: (0.5 * x ^ 0.65)%, where x is the amount of buildings you own.


Bearded Assistants

Cost: 5 Sxd (5e51)

Effect: Increase the production of assistants based on how long their beard is (it grows over time!).

Formula: (x ^ 0.7)%, where x is time spent in this game.

Tier 3 Upgrades

Dwarven Alliance

Cost: 30,000 Dwarven Coins

Effect: Unlocks more Dwarven Upgrades.


Battlehammers

Cost: 500 Sxd (5e53)

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on the amount of Dwarven Coins found in this game.

Formula: (2 * ln(1 + x) ^ 2))%, where x is the amount of Dwarven Coins found in this game.


Magic Resistance

Cost: 5 Spd (5e54)

Effect: Reduce Excavation cost multiplier.

Formula: Reduces cost multiplier by 0.02; with no other reductions applying, the multiplier will be 1.18 instead of 1.2


Overwatch

Cost: 50 Spd (5e55)

Effect: Increase Maximum Mana based on the duration of your longest spell.

Formula: +(50 + 35 * x ^ 0.7), where x is the duration of your longest spell.

Dwarven Heritage

Requirements: Dwarven Champion Trophy

Cost: 75,000 Dwarven Coins

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on the total amount of Excavations you made.

Formula: (2 * x ^ 0.9)%, where x is the number of excavations you made.

Dwarf Research Requirements

Dwarven Forge Quest

Description: Aye again lad! Don't ye want a better place to forge yer weapons? With Enough Blacksmiths me people can help ye!

Requirement: R28 + 3,500 Blacksmiths

Cost: 10 Sxg (1e184)

Effect: Unlocks Dwarven Forges.


Dwarven Forges

Upgrade Blacksmiths to Dwarven Forges, boosting their production based on mana regen and unlocking more unique perks for the building.

Requirements: Dwarven Forge Quest

Cost: 10 USxg (1e187)

Formula: (300 * x ^ 0.6)%, where x is your Mana Regeneration.

Effect: Unlocks Dwarven Unique Buildings.


Earth Core Artifact

Description: This piece of molten rock is continuously shifting its shape, responding to mysterious energy sources.

Requirements: R29 + 2,750th Excavation

Effect: Unlocks The Secrets of the Underworld upgrade.


Secrets of the Underworld

Requirement: Prestige Unique Building, Earth Core Artifact

Cost: 100 Sxg (1e185), 10 M (1e7) Faction Coins of each

Effect: Unlocks Researches for Dwarves and Drow and provides a bonus for individual research branches.

Note: Required once throughout the entire game play and does not count towards the bought upgrade stat.

Note: Secrets of the Underworld allows Prestige factions to benefit from RP production formula with no extra bonus.


Legacy of The Dwarven Kings

Description: It is said that when this horn is blown, the voices of past Dwarven Kings can be heard in the wind.

Requirement: Find the Horn of the Kings artifact (Requires Dwarven Forges and 3250 Excavations)

Cost: 10 SxSpg (1e232), 100 M (1e8) Dwarven Coins

Effect: Adds 3 extra Dwarven research slots: 2 for Craftsmanship and 1 to the faction's facility you are playing.

Note: Required to be bought each Dwarven research run.


Unique Building

Upgraded from Blacksmiths

At R28, the Dwarven Blacksmiths building can be upgraded to a Unique Building: Dwarven Forges, which is required for some challenges and research quests.

Ascension 2 - R116+


High Bastion Quest

Description: Aye lad! Yer castle looks solid, but we think we can do better, yes? Excavate to yer best and then again from the start, we'll have a deal.

Requirement: 5 free excav resets this Reincarnation

Cost: 100 Vg (1e65)

Effect: Grants access to Unique Building.


Unique Building Upgrade

Requirement: High Bastion Quest

Cost: 100 Qivg (1e80)

Effect: Gives High Bastion Unique Building.


Unique Building

Effect: Upgrade Royal Castles to High Bastions, boosting their production based on clicks made in this reincarnation and unlocking more unique perks for the building.

Formula: (0.5 * x ^ 0.5)%, where x is clicks this Reincarnation.

Effect: Grants access to Faction Union.

Tier 4 Upgrades

Dwarven Union

Requirement: High Bastion Unique Building

Cost: 1 Sp (1e24) Dwarven coins

Effect: Grants access to Union Upgrades.

Effect (based on Alignment):

Proof of Order: Increase all building production based on time spent this Reincarnation.

Formula: (3.5 * x ^ 0.65)%, where x is time spent this Reincarnation.


Proof of Chaos: Multiplicatively increase assistant chaotically based on spells cast this Reincarnation.

Formula: ((sum(digit(x) ^ y)) ^ 0.9)%, with (y = floor(log10(1 + x) + 1) / 10 + 2), where x is spells cast this Reincarnation.


Proof of Balance: Increase Mana Regeneration based on clicks made in this Reincarnation.

Formula: (1.5 * ln(1 + x) ^ 2.5)%, where x is amount of clicks made this Reincarnation.


Solidity

Cost: 10 Dtg (1e100)

Effect: Multiplicatively increase production bonus from Gems based on the highest amount of High Bastions you built in this Reincarnation.

Formula: (1.6 * x ^ 0.6)%, where x is the highest amount of High Bastion built in this Reincarnation.


Stonetalking

Cost: 100 Qitg (1e110)

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on the highest between Maximum Mana and Mana Regeneration.

Formula: (2 * ln(1 + max(x, y)) ^ 2)%, where x is Maximum Mana and y is Mana Regeneration.


Refined Minerals

Cost: 1 Notg (1e120)

Effect: Multiplicatively increase Mana Regeneration and Maximum Mana based on highest time spent as Order, Balance or Chaos.

Formula: (0.03 * max(x, y, z) ^ 0.7)%, where x, y & z is time spent as Order, Balance and Chaos in this Reincarnation respectively.

Advanced Dwarven Heritage

Requirement: Dwarf Master trophy and 6 hours activity time (This Game) of Diamond Pickaxe.

Cost: 1 Sp (1e24) Dwarven Coins

Effect: Increase the production of all buildings based on the amount of current excavations and excavation resets. (This Reincarnation).

Formula: (0.025 * (x * (1 + y + z)) ^ 0.65)%, where x is current excavations and y & z are free and ruby excavation resets done in this Reincarnation.

Note: When using their lineage, Advanced Heritage powers are multiplied by the level of the lineage.

Formula: *(1 + 0.3 * level)
