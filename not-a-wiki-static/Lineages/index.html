Lineages

Requirement: R60+

At R60 you will unlock Lineages, which gives you access to powerful upgrades based on your chosen Bloodline in the form of perks, and will even allow you to use the spell of other Factions, provided you complete the respective lineage challenge.

Lineages need 400 Royal Exchanges of the respective Faction plus their Bloodline to be used (needs to be bought every Abdication).

Note: You can not use a Lineage if it matches your faction or its respective Prestige or Elite faction.

Note: Levels only need to be bought once and persist through Abdications and Reincarnations. When ascending, Lineages are set back to a certain level (if higher) - (see Ascension for more info).

There exist 15 different Lineages (1 for every faction except Mercenary, 12 before R130 and 3 more at R130+), with every Lineage having 5 perks plus a Faction Coin boost and in the process giving access to the Grand Champion Trophy.

Base Lineage: Lineage based boost.

Level 5: Perk 1 - Bloodline based boost.

Level 10: Faction specific FC boost.

Level 15: Perk 2 - Unlocks Faction spell (adjusted for your alignment if necessary).

Note: Level 15 Lineage Challenge does not need its Lineage to complete.

Level 20: Perk 3 - Unique Building based boost.

Note: Perk 3 also allows the unlock of Grand Champion trophy.

Level 30 (R105+): Perk 4 - Faction Spell boost.

Level 40 (R139+): Perk 5 - Faction specific boost.


Lineage Menu

Lineage Level Cost

The cost to increase a Lineage level starts at 2.5 B (2.5e9) Faction Coins and each level will cost more than the previous one.

Lineage Cost Formula: 25 * 10 ^ (8 + L + (0.5 * max(0 ; L - 30)) + (0.5 * max(0 ; L - 60)) + (0.5 * max(0 ; L - 90)) + (0.5 * max(0 ; L - 120))) where L is current lineage level.

Lineage Cost Formula Simplified

L0-30: 25 * 10 ^ (8 + L)

L31-60: 25 * 10 ^ (38 + 1.5 * (L - 30))

L61-90: 25 * 10 ^ (83 + 2 * (L - 60))

L91-120: 25 * 10 ^ (143 + 2.5 * (L - 90))

L121-150: 25 * 10 ^ (218 + 3 * (L - 120))


There are two artifacts that affect the Lineage level cost:

Ancient Heirloom (Lore Artifact)

Requirements: Have at least 1 Lineage level purchased.

Effect: Reduces the cost of Lineage levels

Note: Only applies if the level of your current Lineage is lower than the highest Lineage level across all Lineages.

Cost: 10 Dtg (1e100) Diamond Coins, free in A2+

Formula: (x ^ 0.9), where x is the Lineage level cost.


Ancestral Hourglass (Lore Artifact)

Requirement: R100+

Cost: 1 Novg (1e90) Emerald Coins, free in A3+

Effect: Lowers the Lineage cost exponent based on Reincarnations made.

Formula: -(0.01 * R), where R is the number of times you have reincarnated.

Note: Only counts actual Reincarnations, not any "count more" upgrades.
