Quest Artifacts Lore Artifacts

There are 78 Lore Artifacts which combined with other artifacts will unlock different things at different stages of the game.

Attention: Vanilla Faction-specific Lore Artifacts cannot be found if affiliated with a Prestige Faction. (e.g.The Fairy artifacts cannot be found if you are also playing as Dwarven.)

Survey Equipment Upgrade

Requirement: 100 Excavations.

Cost: 100 Dc (1e35)

Effect: Grants access to Lore Artifacts.

Hint: A first-time only discovery.

Description: A common, totally uninteresting stone.

Chance: 2% on the first excavation of a run, after abdication or reincarnation.

Effect: Unlocks Research D290

Hint: Rarely found in the pyramids of old.
